About Harriben Hub

Welcome to Harriben Hub, an independent publication dedicated to sharing practical insights on business, leadership, lifestyle, relationships, and personal development. Our goal is simple: to publish thoughtful, original articles that help readers think more clearly and live more intentionally.

Who Runs This Site

Harriben Hub is owned and edited by Dr. Harrison Onovo (pen name: Harriben). Harrison is a Nigerian writer and entrepreneur with a long-standing interest in leadership, business strategy, and the everyday questions that shape modern family and professional life. Every article on this site is reviewed and edited by him before being published.

What We Cover

Our editorial focus includes:

  • Business & Entrepreneurship — practical guidance for builders, small business owners, and professionals.
  • Leadership & Personal Development — frameworks and reflections drawn from real-life practice.
  • Relationships & Family Life — grounded perspectives on marriage, parenting, and community.
  • Lifestyle, Fashion & Wellness — accessible writing for everyday readers.

Editorial Standards

We commit to publishing original writing. Articles are drafted, reviewed, and edited in-house. We do not republish content from other sites. Where we cite books, research, or third-party sources, we credit them clearly. If you spot an error, please let us know via our contact page and we will correct it promptly.

Get in Touch

You can reach us by email at contactharriben@gmail.com or through our contact page. For professional updates, connect with Harrison on LinkedIn.

Thank you for reading — we are glad you are here.
