Cookie Policy

Last updated: 8 May 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how Harriben Hub uses them, and the choices you have. It should be read together with our Privacy Policy.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website places on your device (computer, phone, or tablet) when you visit it. They are widely used to make websites work, to make them work more efficiently, and to provide information to the site owner.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the Site to function. They handle things such as cross-site request forgery (CSRF) protection and session management. Without them, parts of the Site (like submitting forms) would not work.

Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors interact with the Site, which pages are most popular, and how readers find us. The data is generally aggregated and does not identify you personally. You can learn more in Google's cookies overview.

Advertising Cookies

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google and its partners use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. This is sometimes referred to as personalised, behavioural, or interest-based advertising. These cookies may also be used to measure the effectiveness of ads and to limit how often you see the same ad.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies are set by services that appear on our pages (for example, Google Analytics and Google AdSense). We do not control how these third parties use the cookies they set. You should review their respective policies:

  • Google Privacy & Terms
  • How Google uses cookies in advertising

4. How to Control Cookies

You have several ways to control or limit how cookies are used:

  • Most browsers let you refuse or delete cookies via their settings. Disabling essential cookies may break parts of the Site.
  • You can opt out of personalised advertising from Google by visiting Google's Ads Settings.
  • For broader opt-out options, visit www.aboutads.info/choices or www.youronlinechoices.com.

5. Changes to This Cookie Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as our practices and applicable laws evolve. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent change.

6. Contact

If you have questions about our use of cookies, please contact us at contactharriben@gmail.com.
